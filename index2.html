About Me

Hello. My name is Austin Williams. I was born in Jacksonville, Fl and moved to Spring Hill, TN in sixth grade. I attended Middle Tennessee State University, and majored in athletic training. Since graduation, I have worked in all levels of sport, from the NFL, NCAA football, USL, and high school. Fun fact: my first job was in Birmingham, Al where I covered a local high school called Hueytown. At Hueytown, we had a junior quarterback named Jameis Winston-some may have heard of him. Most recently I was the clinical coordinator of event medicine at Vanderbilt University Medical Center. In this role, I oversaw a staff of 25 part-time athletic trainers. We were contracted with over 40 teams, leagues, schools, etc. that requested sports medicine coverage. I would staff as well as provide equipment and supplies, for all events. My wife and I were married in 2013 and moved to Nashville. We have a two year old daughter. I love all things sports. I am a die hard Florida Gators and Jacksonville Jaguars fan. I enjoy playing golf, being outside, going to concerts, and spending time with friends and family. Coding school, thus far, has been a huge challenge, but enjoying the process and work that goes into it. I look forward to growing with everyone over these next 3 months.
